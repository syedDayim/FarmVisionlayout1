About Us

Pioneering Sustainable
Horticulture

At Farm Vision, our mission is to empower farmers in Jammu and Kashmir by transforming traditional horticulture into modern, high-density apple orchards. We partner with local farmers, providing high-yielding trees and essential support to elevate productivity to global standards. Committed to innovation and sustainability, we strive to make Jammu and Kashmir a leader in apple production through comprehensive services, from orchard design to post-planting management.

Innovation-Driven

Our cutting-edge methods and sustainable practices set new standards in the agricultural industry, driving growth and success for farmers.

Orchard Excellence

We provide expert advice and comprehensive support throughout the orchard lifecycle, from planting to harvest, to ensure your trees flourish and produce outstanding results.

Our Services

Designing the Future of Apple Orchards

Crafting Excellence in Every Orchard. Our services cover every stage, ensuring your orchard thrives with cutting-edge techniques and sustainable practices.

High-Density Orchard Planning & Design

We offer expert consultation and customized design services to create ultra high-density apple orchards tailored to your land’s unique characteristics, maximizing productivity and sustainability.


Supply of High-Yielding Trees

We provide top-quality apple trees on advanced rootstock sourced from Europe’s leading nurseries, ensuring your orchard’s success from the very start.



Orchard Infrastructure Development

From trellising systems to irrigation solutions, we supply and install all necessary support structures to ensure optimal growth and ease of management.


Training & Technical Support

We empower farmers with the knowledge and skills needed to manage modern orchards through hands-on training and ongoing technical support, ensuring your long-term success.

Post-Planting Management

Our services don’t stop at planting. We offer guidance on pruning, pest management, and overall orchard maintenance to help you achieve the highest yields possible

Our Mission

Cultivating a Sustainable Tomorrow

  • Our vision reaches beyond just growing apple trees; it’s about nurturing the connection between people, land, and tradition. Grounded in sustainability and self-reliance, we aim to empower growers with knowledge and resources to flourish in a changing world.
  • We’re not just increasing yields; we’re fostering resilience and prosperity within agricultural communities. By integrating global best practices with local wisdom, we’re planting the seeds for a future of abundance that will endure for generations. With us, you’re not just growing trees; you’re cultivating a legacy.
  • At Farm Vision our goal is to be a one-stop solution for farmers, helping them achieve global competitiveness. While European farmers achieve yields of 50 tons per hectare, our mission is to elevate Kashmir’s farmers to the same level of success.
  • With deep roots in Kashmir’s agricultural heritage and a focus on innovation, we are not just in the business of selling apple trees; we are transforming lives and communities through sustainable agriculture.

From the Founder

Building close relationships with our farmers and partners is at the heart of our mission. We believe that true progress in horticulture comes from collaboration, trust, and a shared vision. From the initial concept of an orchard to its full bloom, we stand by our stakeholders, nurturing these connections long after the trees have taken root. Together, we are cultivating a future where every orchard thrives, and every farmer prospers

Ehsan Javaid
